# 3) *Laser* panel

![panel](design-3.jpg)

This panel is composed of:
- a rocket switch (`P3_SW2_0`) to arm the laser
- a 2-position toggle-switch (`P3_SW2_1`) for the color (red/blue)

## Connections

| Switch     | function | TM | Line | Pin |
|:----------:|:--------:|:--:|:----:|:---:|
| `P3_SW2_0` | armement | 1  |  K3  | 6   |
| `P3_SW2_1` | color    | 1  |  K3  | 7   |
